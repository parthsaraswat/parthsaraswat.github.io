About, briefly

I’m a hardware engineer working in San Francisco. I also produce a podcast, and write about my learnings in tech and life regularly.

I grew up taking things apart and not always putting them back together. That habit eventually became a degree, then a job, then the ongoing practice of writing down what I learned so I wouldn’t have to learn it twice.

These days I work on hardware in San Francisco. The rest of my time goes to a podcast I produce, a handful of essays each month, and the slow, happy project of becoming the kind of person who answers emails.

If you’d like to talk about a board, a book, or a bad idea that might be a good one, set up a time to chat.
